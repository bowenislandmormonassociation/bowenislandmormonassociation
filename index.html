Bowen Island Mormon Association

The Bowen Island Mormon Association (BIMA) was founded in 1978 to support members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints on Bowen Island, British Columbia. Starting with a small group of families, BIMA has grown into a central resource, hosting weekly worship gatherings, family activities, and study groups focused on faith and community service.

About Us

BIMA organizes a range of annual events, from outdoor retreats and family history workshops to food drives and service projects, fostering a strong bond among members and a spirit of giving back to the Bowen Island community. Set amid the island’s beautiful landscapes, these gatherings help members deepen their faith while connecting with one another.

Today, BIMA remains committed to creating an inclusive, welcoming environment for anyone interested in learning more about the church. With a focus on faith, service, and friendship, the Bowen Island Mormon Association continues to be a pillar of support for Bowen Island’s Latter-day Saint community.
